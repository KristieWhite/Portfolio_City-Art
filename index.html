ABOUT ME

In 2013, I purchased CS6 Design & Web and subscribed to Creative Cloud (still have them), and have been geeking away with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, InDesign, HTML5 & CSS3. Playing with Adobe software and coding was so much fun, I decided to take it to a new level and learn Front End Development. After self teaching here and there, I enrolled at The Iron Yard in the Front End Engineering course. It was a 3 month immersive boot camp and they taught a lot of code! School projects included HTML5, CSS3 (Fluid Grid and Media Queries), JQuery, AJAX, JavaScript, Git, Mustache, Backbone and React with Webpack.

In pursuit of my new career, I am looking forward to working with a Senior Developer to expand my skills. I am flexible in which direction to go, as I enjoy doing different things. I believe the sky is the limit when it comes to code... and just about everything! From something specialized like mobile device development, wearable devices, games, or making a desktop website, all is good!
