About Me

In 2013, I purchased CS6 Design & Web and subscribed to Creative Cloud (still have them), and have been geeking away with Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, HTML5 & CSS3. Playing with Adobe and code was so much fun, I decided to take it to a new level and learn programming. After self teaching here and there for about a year, I enrolled at The Iron Yard in the Front End Engineering course. It was a 3 month immersive boot camp and they taught a lot of code! As I continue to learn as much as possible with both Adobe software and code, I will be adding more projects to my portfolio and GitHub.
